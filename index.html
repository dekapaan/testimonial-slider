About Me

I am an introvert, but that does not affect my confidence and skills. I am geared towards helping those around me and tend to take up that role without actively seeking it outeffort. I am a critical and analytical thinker to form accurate judgment, and these skills fuel my ability to learn and implement new concepts. I usually do not put myself in a leadershipin leadership position, but if the need arises I am able to meet the role head-on. I have run a backyard nursery and have done freelance work in the past. This has given me valuable skills such as communication skills and the ability to delegate tasks.

My interest in this field came from the fact that I enjoy self-sufficiency and prefer to solve problems by building the necessary skills as needed. This has caused me to gain experience in working with the terminal and troubleshooting problems in particular to phones and computers. I have also played around with loading different Linux distributions for old phones to improve performance.
